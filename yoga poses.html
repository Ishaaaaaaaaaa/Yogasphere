Popular Yoga Poses

Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

One of the most popular poses, this strengthens the entire body and improves flexibility.

Benefits: Strengthens arms, legs, and core. Stretches the back and hamstrings.

Warrior Pose (Virabhadrasana)

Warrior Pose is great for building strength in the legs and enhancing balance.

Benefits: Improves leg strength, stability, and core engagement.

Child's Pose (Balasana)

A restful pose that stretches the back, hips, and relieves tension in the body.

Benefits: Relieves back and neck tension, promotes relaxation.

Tree Pose (Vrksasana)

A balancing pose that improves concentration and strengthens the legs.

Benefits: Improves balance, strengthens legs and core, enhances focus.

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

This pose helps in stretching the chest, back, and shoulders while improving posture.

Benefits: Opens the chest, strengthens the back, and improves posture.
